About

I work at the intersection of AI safety and evidence-based policy.

My research focuses on the translation gap between technical safety findings and the governance systems that must act on them. Previously, I worked across the UN and World Bank ecosystems, developing fragility indices, early-warning systems, and model-driven policy simulations.

These experiences taught me how to build technical tools that are rigorous for analysts and legible for decision-makers—a skillset I now apply to AI governance and policy.
